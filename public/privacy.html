Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to VedAI. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you sign in, we collect your email address and display name via Google Authentication or Email method.
  • Chat Data: We store your chat history and messages to provide the service and allow you to access past conversations.
  • Uploaded Content: Any images or files you upload are processed solely for the purpose of the chat interaction.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate your identity.
  • Provide AI responses via our third-party provider (Groq).
  • Sync your chat history across devices using Supabase.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a secure database platform. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Groq: For generating AI responses.
  • Supabase: For database and authentication services.
  • Google OAuth: For secure sign-in.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. You can delete your chat history at any time within the application settings.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
